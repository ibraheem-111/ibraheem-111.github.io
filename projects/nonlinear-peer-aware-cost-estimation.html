Nonlinear Peer-Aware Cost Estimation

Research project on nonlinear cost-driven robot navigation under real-time sensing constraints.

$cat case-study.md

Summary

Aug 2025 - Ongoing | GHOST Lab, ASU

Built a control stack where a mobile robot tracks behavior generated by a nonlinear peer-aware objective.

What I built

  • Motion-capture calibration for real-time rigid body localization.
  • ROS nodes for control and feedback logging.
  • EKF-based filtering for noisy mocap measurements.
  • MPC loop for nonlinear trajectory behavior.
  • Bayesian intent inference integration for belief-aware decisions.

Results

  • Stable closed-loop control with filtered state estimates.
  • Improved tracking reliability relative to raw sensor-only operation.
  • An IJRR manuscript submission is currently in progress.
  • Additional quantitative metrics are being finalized.
