Summary

Nov 2025 - Dec 2025 | Arizona State University

Implemented a full stack where language instructions are parsed, planned, and executed through ROS2 action pipelines and vision-based object perception.

What I built

  • NLP parsing from commands to task intents.
  • ROS2 orchestrator for sequencing perception, grasp, and place actions.
  • Stereo perception for object size, color, and depth estimation.
  • Retry and verification loops for failed manipulations.

Results

  • Reliable command-to-action execution in lab task setups.
  • Functional 3D perception pipeline for block handling tasks.
  • Further benchmark results can be published after final cleanup.
